Passports and Visas

Passports

Here's what you need to know about applying for a passport:

  • You need to provide proof of citizenship and identity.
  • You need to submit a recent passport photo.
  • The application fee is $110.
  • Passports are typically processed within 4-6 weeks.

Visas

Here's what you need to know about applying for a visa:

  • You need to provide proof of the purpose of your trip.
  • You may need to provide proof of financial support.
  • The application fee varies depending on the country.
  • Visa processing times vary depending on the country.
